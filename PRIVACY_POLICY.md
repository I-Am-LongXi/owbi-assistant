# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 6, 2026

**Owbi Assistant** ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application collects, uses, handles, and shares your information. By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our voice assistant functionality:

- **Microphone / Audio Data:** To provide voice input and voice call features, the app requests access to your device's microphone (`RECORD_AUDIO` permission). We only record audio when you actively interact with the voice features of the app.
- **Network Data:** The app requires internet access to connect to the self-hosted server URL you specify in the settings.

We **do not** collect telemetry, crash reports, analytics, or any background location data.

## 2. How We Use and Handle Your Information
The audio data collected by the microphone is processed **entirely ephemerally**. It is captured by the internal web browser (WebView) and transmitted **directly and exclusively** to the Open WebUI server URL that you configure. 

- **No Third-Party Transmission:** We **do not** collect, store, transmit, or monetize your audio data on any servers controlled by us or any third-party advertisers or analytics providers.
- **Security:** Audio data transmission is secured by your device's native WebRTC and HTTPS protocols, provided the server URL you configure supports secure connections.

## 3. Data Retention and Deletion
Because our app acts solely as a client wrapper and transmits your data directly to your designated Open WebUI server:
- **The Android app itself does not retain or store any of your audio data persistently.** 
- To request the deletion of your voice data or chat history, you must contact the administrator of the specific Open WebUI instance you are connected to, or manage it directly through your Open WebUI account interface.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our application is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided personal information to the self-hosted servers they connect to via this app, please take the necessary actions on that server to remove it.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
