# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 3, 2026

**Owbi Assistant** ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application collects, uses, and shares your information.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Audio Data:** When you use the voice input or voice call features, the app requests access to your device's microphone (`RECORD_AUDIO` permission).
- **Network Data:** The app requires internet access to connect to the server URL you specify.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- The audio data collected by the microphone is processed entirely ephemerally. It is captured by the internal web browser (WebView) and sent **directly to the Open WebUI server URL that you configure in the app's settings**.
- We **do not** collect, store, transmit, or monetize your audio data on any third-party servers controlled by us. Your data remains strictly between your device and the server you choose to connect to.

## 3. Third-Party Services
This app acts as a client wrapper for Open WebUI. The processing of your voice data and chat history is governed by the privacy practices of the specific Open WebUI instance you connect to. 

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
